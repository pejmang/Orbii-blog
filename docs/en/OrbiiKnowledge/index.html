Orbii Knowledge - Version 1.0: Join the Contributor Community

Orbii Knowledge is a fully open-source platform, created by Pejman Gohari, with the valuable help of Armin Kiany, CEO of EdClub. Available on GitHub, Orbii Knowledge focuses on sharing knowledge in a simple and effective way, through a minimalist interface.

This platform is in its initial version and still needs to evolve with new features. However, it already offers a solid foundation for publishing articles with a focus on simplicity, speed, and accessibility. Based on the 11ty (Eleventy) static site generator, Orbii Knowledge is easy to customize and use for any type of content.

Whether you're a developer, content architect, or a knowledge-sharing enthusiast, join a dynamic community and contribute to improving Orbii Knowledge. We believe in collective power to evolve this platform into something greater.

You will have the opportunity to bring your ideas, develop new features, and enhance the user experience. Joining Orbii Knowledge means being part of an open-source project accessible to all, where every contribution matters.

Why Contribute?

By contributing to Orbii Knowledge, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Take part in the evolution of a platform used by an international community of content creators.
  • Enhance existing features or propose new ones to meet the community's needs.
  • Connect with developers and creators sharing the same goals.
  • Work with modern technologies like 11ty, Node.js, and continuous integration tools.

Your contribution can make a real difference in the future of Orbii Knowledge and open new possibilities in the world of simple and accessible blogging.

How to Contribute to Orbii Knowledge?

Whether you want to contribute to the source code, design templates, or optimize performance, we need your skills. Here are the various ways you can participate:

  • Feature Development: Help us add new features or improve existing ones.
  • Performance Optimization: Contribute to making the platform even faster and more responsive by improving front-end or back-end performance.
  • Design and Customization: Create new templates or improve existing designs to enhance user experience.
  • Documentation Writing: Help write clear and comprehensive guides to make the platform easier to use for other users and contributors.

Contribution Process

Contributing to Orbii Knowledge is simple and accessible. Here's how you can get started:

  1. Clone the Orbii Knowledge GitHub repository from this link.
  2. Create a new branch for your contribution to work safely.
  3. Make your changes and submit a pull request explaining the changes you've made.
  4. Your contribution will be reviewed by our team before being merged into the main project.

We look forward to seeing what you will bring to Orbii Knowledge, and we thank you for your participation in this open-source project.

Join Us

If you have ideas, suggestions, or questions, feel free to contact us or open a ticket on our GitHub repository. Together, let's make Orbii Knowledge an even more powerful and accessible platform for everyone.
