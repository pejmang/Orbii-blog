Makers

**Makers** are visionary leaders who drive digital transformation while staying deeply connected to the operational realities of the company. These leaders don’t just set ambitious strategies; they are actively involved in both innovation and execution. In a world shaped by AI, their role is even more critical as they must understand not only market dynamics but also the technological implications of new tools. They orchestrate teams and projects while fostering an environment that encourages experimentation and innovation.
