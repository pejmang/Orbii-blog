Velocity

Agility, resilience, and execution speed to adapt to the ecosystem in the best possible conditions and within tight deadlines. Speed is one of the most critical capabilities for an organization aiming to lead in the age of AI. It not only fosters faster innovation but also enables adaptation to crises and disruptions with resilience. High-performing companies adopt agile processes that allow them to react swiftly to market changes, develop products and services more rapidly, and stay ahead of the competition. This velocity is made possible by the synergy of the other pillars, enabling the organization to execute its strategies with unmatched precision and speed.

Building Resilience in a Complex Business World
The business world of the last 10 years has gone from evolution to revolution with a profound metamorphosis which demonstrates for some that our world has become even more VUCA than ever. Indeed, in this VUCA world, projects, priorities, strategies, markets, environments change quickly, and it is necessary, even vital, to be able to adapt quickly.
