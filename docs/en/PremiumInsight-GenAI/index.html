State of the Art of Generative AI: Technological Perspective and Pragmatic Insights

Published: Nov 2024

Abstract:
This study explores the forefront of Generative AI, focusing on the evolution and advancements of Large Language Models and their transformative impact on natural language processing. It examines Retrieval-Augmented Generation methodologies, including GraphRAG, which enhance AI accuracy by integrating external data sources. Technological innovations such as omni models, Mixture of Experts and advancements in video and voice models are highlighted for their role in optimizing multimodal data processing and computational efficiency. The analysis underscores the strategic importance of leveraging cloud-based AI infrastructure from major providers to balance scalability and cost. Additionally, it addresses essential AI development frameworks and robust data management strategies, including data collection, storage, governance, and bias mitigation. Maximizing model and data efficiency through data-centric AI, curriculum learning, and model distillation is discussed to promote sustainable AI solutions. Privacy and safety measures, including machine unlearning and toxicity mitigation, are emphasized as critical for ethical AI deployment. This comprehensive overview provides X-Tech companies with actionable insights to effectively harness Generative AI’s potential, optimize infrastructure and data strategies, and maintain a competitive edge in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.
